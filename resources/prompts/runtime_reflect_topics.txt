TOPIC POLICY

`WORLD.topics` is a compact retrieval index for future bootstrap memory recall.
It is not a chat summary.

Good topics are:
- stable user themes
- ongoing project or domain themes
- recurring areas of interest
- durable conversation threads likely to matter again

Bad topics are:
- greetings
- one-turn phrasings
- tiny wording variations
- generic meta-chatter with no retrieval value

Topic stability rules:
- preserve topic continuity across short replies like "yes", "okay", and acknowledgements
- prefer small edits over full replacement
- do not leave `WORLD.topics` empty when durable recurring topics are clearly visible
